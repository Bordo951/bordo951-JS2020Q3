What can this calculator do?

  • Mathematical operations such as addition (+), subtraction (-), multiplication (*), division (÷).
  • Square root (√) and exponentiation (x - specify the number, click on the button and enter the degree).
  • Performs with negative numbers (to perform an operation with a negative number, enter the desired number and then press the button ±).
  • Works with fractional numbers (for example: 0.2, etc).
